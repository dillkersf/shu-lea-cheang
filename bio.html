SHU LEA CHEANG

★ARTIST★

Shu Lea Cheang is a Taiwanese-American artist and filmmaker. She got a BA in history from the National Taiwan University and an MA in Cinema Studies from New York University in 1979. Cheang lived and worked in New York in the 80s-90s. She joined the collective Paper Tiger Television and started producing live weekly programs that used public-access channels to reach cable subscribers. She now lives in Paris. She has navigated topics of ethnic stereotyping, sexual politics, and institutional oppression with her art. She is a multimedia and new-media artist. She also represented Taiwan at the 58th Venice Biennale in 2019. She is the first woman to represent Taiwan with a solo-exhibition.
